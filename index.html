❓ Why run diagnostics offline?

Most crop disease diagnoses are required out in the fields, vineyards, or orchards where cellular data networks (3G/4G/5G) are weak or unavailable. This tool compiles the entire neural network and treatments dictionary on-device using WebAssembly, ensuring it operates perfectly anywhere without an internet connection.

❓ How to take accurate diagnostic photos?

Align the affected region of the leaf directly within the central dotted Target Frame. Avoid holding the camera at an angle. Lay the leaf flat on a clean surface or hold it straight. Ensure that the region is fully in focus, and there are no dark shadows or harsh direct sunshine/glare reflections on the leaf.
